Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Here are answers to some of the most common things people ask us.

How long does computer repair take?

Many common repairs can be completed the same day or within 24–48 hours, depending on parts and the complexity of the issue. We will always give you an estimated time once we complete the free diagnostic.

Do you provide a warranty for repairs?

Yes. We offer a 30-day warranty on our repair work and replacement parts. If the same issue returns within 30 days, contact us and we will review it at no extra diagnostic cost.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, major credit and debit cards, and common digital payment methods. If you have questions about payment, let us know and we’ll work with you.
